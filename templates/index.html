About

The AirDispatch project was started in an attempt to fix the problems with the email system; however, since then the idea has grown into something much larger. AirDispatch can be a distributed database for users that provides messaging between services.

Philosophy

The internet is changing rapidly. We have moved from a time where people developed open and secure methods of sharing data to an era of entrepreneurship and segregation. Data is being kept in large islands without any chance for interoperability. We are aiming to fix that.

With AirDispatch, users can finally be free from the clutches of centralized services.
